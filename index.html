The Genesis

"Banjara Charithamrutha" (ಬಂಜಾರಾ ಚರಿತಾಮೃತ) is a monumental literary work that documents the complete history, culture, traditions, and way of life of the Banjara (Lambadi/Lamani) community — one of India's most vibrant and culturally rich communities. Born from years of meticulous research and deep personal connection, this book stands as a definitive cultural archive.

🏛️

Cultural Preservation

Just as every tree in nature possesses its own unique personality, so too does every community carry its own distinct qualities and traditions. Among the many communities of India, the Banjara people stand apart — their clothing, ornaments, songs, and lifestyle are remarkably different and special. This book meticulously preserves these traditions from the origins of the community to the present day.

🌺

A Living Heritage

While many traditions across India are gradually disappearing, certain communities retain their unique identity against all odds. The Banjara community is one such special group. From their elaborate gotra system to their sacred rituals, from their distinctive attire to their unique dialect — this book covers every facet of Banjara life, compiled from birth to death customs and everything in between.

Santosh Shivalala Rathod

Author's Introduction (ಲೇಖಕರ ಪರಿಚಯ)

Greetings to all my dear relatives and readers. With great joy, I introduce myself to you today. My name is Santosh Shivalala Rathod, born on 5th March 1986 to Shivalala Rathod and Mutlabai Rathod in Aluhor, L.T. No. 1, Indi Taluk, Vijayapura District, Karnataka.

Education & Career

Growing up was a journey of resilience. Despite facing hardships and witnessing the struggles of family and community, the pursuit of knowledge never wavered. Completing B.Sc (PCM) from Vijayapur DFA, followed by Adarsh College, Hesaraghatta, Bengaluru for D-Pharmacy, and then BSc in Nautical Science from MAM College, Gulbarga, Chennai — the academic journey culminated in serving as a Merchant Navy Officer.

The Creative Spirit

Beyond the seas, a creative fire burned within. Working in the cinema and film industry as a writer and director, Santosh channeled his artistic vision to inspire the youth. The desire to preserve and document his community's heritage, customs, and traditions led to years of dedicated research, drawing inspiration from gurus like Vikram Nandayya Gol and Swetarada Raju Madhubhaviyavara. This monumental effort resulted in the book that stands before you today — "Banjara Charithamrutha".

"I request this book to reach the hearts and minds of all. May it serve as a bridge connecting the Banjara community to its glorious past and promising future."
— Santosh Shivalala Rathod

ಸ್ವಾಗತ Welcome

You are about to explore the rich and vibrant world of the Banjara community — one of India's most culturally distinct and colorful communities. Known as Lambadi, Lamani, or Banjara, these nomadic people have carried their traditions across centuries, adorning India's cultural tapestry with their music, art, jewelry, and an unbreakable spirit of unity.

This website celebrates "Banjara Charithamrutha" — a literary masterpiece documenting their complete heritage.
